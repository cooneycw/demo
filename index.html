The Story

A historic sweep 46 years in the making

On February 22, 2026 — the 46th anniversary of the Miracle on Ice — the United States men's hockey team defeated Canada 2-1 in overtime to capture gold at the Milan-Cortina Olympics. Three days earlier, the women's team had done the exact same thing: USA 2, Canada 1, overtime. For the first time in history, both American hockey teams stood atop the Olympic podium at the same Games.
